Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). This policy complies with Google Play Store requirements, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and general data protection best practices.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation, authentication, and communication)
  • User ID (a unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication)

Note: We do not store passwords. All authentication is handled securely by Firebase Authentication.

1.2 Financial & Expense Data

  • Expense entries (amount, category, description, date)
  • Currency information (original and selected base currency)
  • Exchange rates used at the time of calculation
  • Budgets, limits, and spending categories
  • Aggregated financial analytics

1.3 Local Device Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using a local database (Hive). This data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

1.4 Cloud Data (When Logged In)

When logged in, your data is securely synchronized with Firebase Firestore to enable backup, recovery, and multi-device access.

1.5 App Usage & Diagnostics

  • App version and device type
  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Aggregated usage statistics (Firebase Analytics)

1.6 Advertising Data

The App may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and interaction data. You can control ad personalization through your device settings.

1.7 Notifications

Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time through the App or your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide expense tracking features
  • Sync and back up your data
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements (if enabled)
