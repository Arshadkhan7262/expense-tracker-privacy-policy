Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Password: Stored securely using Firebase Authentication (encrypted and hashed)
  • User ID: Generated by Firebase Authentication to uniquely identify your account

1.2 Financial Data

To provide expense tracking functionality, we collect:

  • Expense Records: Amount, category, description, date, and currency information
  • Budget Information: Budget limits and categories you set
  • Currency Preferences: Your selected base currency and exchange rate data
  • Financial Analytics: Spending patterns, trends, and statistics calculated from your expense data

Important: All financial data is stored locally on your device using Hive database and synchronized to Firebase Firestore (cloud storage) when you are logged in. Your expense data includes original amounts, original currencies, exchange rates at creation time, and base currency conversions to ensure accurate historical records.

1.3 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information for app functionality:

  • Device Type: Android/iOS device information
  • App Version: To provide updates and bug fixes
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and stack traces via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics and usage statistics

1.4 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App:

  • App Interactions: Features you use, screens you visit, and actions you take
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage statistics via Firebase Analytics
  • Remote Configuration: Feature flags and app settings managed through Firebase Remote Config

1.5 Advertising Data

When you use the App, we may collect:

  • Advertising ID: Google AdMob advertising identifier for personalized ads
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads you view or interact with
  • Device Information: Used by AdMob to serve relevant advertisements

Note: Ad personalization can be controlled through your device's advertising settings. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

1.6 Notification Data

To provide notification services, we may access:

  • Notification Permissions: Your consent to receive notifications
  • Notification Preferences: Settings for expense reminders, budget alerts, and spending notifications
  • Device Notification Settings: To deliver notifications according to your preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Store and synchronize your expense data across devices
  • Calculate spending statistics, budgets, and financial analytics
  • Provide multi-currency support and exchange rate conversions
  • Generate reports, charts, and visualizations of your spending

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs through crash reporting
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Optimize app performance and stability

2.3 Communication

  • Send you important updates about the App
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send expense reminders and budget alerts (if enabled)
  • Notify you about spending patterns and financial insights

2.4 Advertising

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Control ad frequency and placement through Firebase Remote Config

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your expense data is stored locally on your device using Hive database. This includes:

  • All expense records
  • Budget information
  • User preferences and settings
  • Cached authentication credentials (encrypted)

Security: Local data is stored in your device's secure storage area. Authentication credentials are stored using Base64 encoding and are only used for automatic login convenience.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)

When you are logged in, your data is synchronized to Firebase Firestore, Google's cloud database service. This enables:

  • Data backup and recovery
  • Multi-device synchronization
  • Offline data access

Security: Firebase Firestore uses industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account only.

3.3 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Firestore database, Crashlytics, Analytics, and Remote Config
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services
  • Exchange Rate APIs: For currency conversion (exchange rates are fetched but not stored permanently)

These services have their own privacy policies and data processing practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy
