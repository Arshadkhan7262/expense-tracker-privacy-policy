Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Expense Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the App). This policy complies with Google Play Store requirements, GDPR, CCPA, and general data protection best practices.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email Address – used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • User ID – a unique identifier generated and managed by Firebase Authentication

Note: We do not store passwords. Authentication is handled securely by Firebase Authentication.

1.2 Financial & Expense Data

  • Expense entries (amount, category, description, date)
  • Currency information (original currency, selected base currency)
  • Exchange rates used at the time of calculation
  • Budgets, limits, and spending categories
  • Aggregated financial analytics

1.3 Local Device Storage

Data is stored locally on your device using a local database (Hive). This data remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.

1.4 Cloud Data (When Logged In)

When logged in, your data is securely synchronized with Firebase Firestore for backup, recovery, and multi-device access.

1.5 App Usage & Diagnostics

  • App version and device type
  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Aggregated usage statistics (Firebase Analytics)

1.6 Advertising Data

The App may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising IDs and interaction data. You can control ad personalization through your device settings.

1.7 Notifications

Notifications are optional and can be disabled anytime through app or device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide expense tracking features
  • Sync and back up your data
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Improve app performance
  • Display ads (if enabled)

3. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission, secure authentication, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob
  • Exchange rate providers (conversion only)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only when required to operate the App or comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, edit, or delete your data
  • Delete your account from App settings
  • Control notifications and ads

7. Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your account from Settings → Delete Account or by contacting us. Cloud data is deleted within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, including the United States.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means acceptance of updates.
